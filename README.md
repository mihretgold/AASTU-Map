## About The Project

The AASTU University Map Application aims to provide students, faculty, staff, and visitors with a comprehensive digital map of the university campus. This application will facilitate easy navigation, location finding, and exploration of campus facilities.

Whether it's a student seeking their next class location, a faculty member needing to find a meeting venue, a staff member looking for a specific department, or a visitor eager to explore the campus landmarks, the AASTU University Map Application is designed to cater to diverse navigation needs with efficiency and precision.
